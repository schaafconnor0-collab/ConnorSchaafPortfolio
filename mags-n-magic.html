Mags & Magic

Hero Based Shooter

Video Overview

Multiplayer FPS

The video displays the abilities of our three developed playable heroes. Each hero has their own unique key binded abilities as well as a unique passive ability.


The Vampire King has two key binded abilities. The first is "Bloodfiend dash" which gives the user a speed boost but costs 25 health. The Vampire King also has an ability called "Dark Whisper" that pulls an enemy player toward the user. The cooldown changes based on whether the ability connected or not. The passive ability for the Vampire Kings is that if the user has not been hit for 5 seconds they will begin regenerating health at 5 per second.


The Fairy also has two key binded abilities. The first ability is known as "pixie dust" which throws a flashbang that deals 30 damage and temporarily stuns enemies. The second ability is known as "Magic Charm" which throws out a projectile that resets the cooldowns of all enemy abilities. The passive for the fairy allows her to float when the user presses left shift.


The Elementalist has one constant ability which is "swap". When this ability is used, the user switches from one element to the other. The elementalist has water and fire mode. When in water mode the user gains a "vortex" ability which creates a water orb around the player that sucks players into in and deals 90 damage on contact. When in fire mode, the user can throw a "fireball" that does 50 damage on contact and then does an excess 10 damage per second for each second someone is in the explosion radius. When in fire mode, the user also has a passibe where enemies take 5 damage per second when within 5 meters of the user.

My Personal Contribution

The biggest thing that I individually worked on in this game was the multiplayer system. To expand, I worked on the systems to get four players into a lobby and be able to interact with one and other in real time. I also implemented catch and save systems to prevent small errors in this process from crashing the game, and allow for numerous servers to be active at once.


I also did the majority of the work on the Vampire and Fairy characters. In specific I imoplemented all of the abilities and passives for the Vampire, and I implemeted the flight and Magic Charm abilities for the fairy.


The other major gameplay feature that I made was the leaderboard system. This included allowing players to name themselves and track other players kills across each players screen. This was also essential for implementing a bounty system so that we could track in the back end which players were on the biggest streaks and which players needed some help.
